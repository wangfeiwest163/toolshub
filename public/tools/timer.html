How to Use

Stopwatch:
  • Click "Start" to begin timing
  • Click "Pause" to pause the stopwatch
  • Click "Reset" to clear the time
  • Click "Lap" to record lap times
  • Press SPACEBAR to start/pause the timer
Timer:
  • Set hours, minutes, and seconds
  • Click "Start" to begin countdown
  • Timer will alert when complete
  • Select alarm sound for notification
